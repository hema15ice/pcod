Understanding PCOD

Important facts every woman should know

Prevalence

PCOD affects approximately 1 in 10 women of reproductive age, making it one of the most common endocrine disorders.

Common Symptoms

Irregular periods, excessive hair growth, acne, weight gain, and fertility issues are among the most frequent symptoms.

Long-term Health

Early diagnosis and management can reduce risks of type 2 diabetes, heart disease, and other complications.
